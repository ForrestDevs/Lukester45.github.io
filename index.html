For the Adventurous

Built for the adventurous by the adventurous, Gear Shed takes your large gear closet and organizes it into one place. Have a new perspective on your outrageous amount of gear, document, track, record and plan your adventures with new insight.

Build

Add gear to your new digital Gear Shed. Categorize your Gear by shelf and brand. Record weight, price, purchase date and details. Tag gear by favourites, wishes and regrets.

Create

Create new adventures and activities. Add your Gear to a new list. Plan out piles of gear, packing organization and create gear diaries, recording important information or suggestions for use next time.

Share

Share categorized gear, created adventures or activities. Beautiful, simple, clear PDFs to send to friends or fellow trip companions.
